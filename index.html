Get started with the National Urban League Financial Empowerment Program

Thousands of Dashers like you have signed up for free access to goalup.org - a free platform we created in partnership with the National Urban league to help you achieve your financial goals. GoalUp combines the Urban League’s proven financial literacy educational programming with an accessible, mobile-friendly online format.

With GoalUp, you can access on-demand financial literacy courses across a range of topics from savings and credit to auto-financing and budgeting. As this partnership continues, the content library will continue to expand with modules on entrepreneurship, workforce training, and more!
